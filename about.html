About Us

Welcome To '' Nature Agro Resort ''

Nothing's better than this...


Nature Agro Resort is an exotic destination for travellers, adventurers, and foodies, located just a few kilometres away from the city of rivers, Sangamner, Maharashtra. Nature Agro Resort is an excellent combination of superb accommodations, tasty food, and stunning landmark for netizens

Nature Agro Resort is the first ecotel (eco-friendly hotel) in Sangamner tehsil which is managed by hotelier Mr Vijay Thorat. He started his entrepreneurial journey by establishing

Nature Agro Resort in 2018..

Since then nature lovers and highway travellers have shown their love and support to the Nature Agro Resort unconditionally. Nature is known across the state for its special traditional black curry recipes. Nature lovers visit the resort just to have a great meal.

Surrounded by various plants and trees, for nature lovers, the stay seems like a paradise. The cold breeze, and fresh air, make the stay at the resort even more memorable.

Just like our customers, honeybees, hummingbirds, house sparrows, and wild animals are our usual visitors. We believe in coexistence, that’s why our goal is to make Nature Agro Resort a great source of inspiration for our customers to realize that, if we protect nature, nature will protect us.

Words aren’t enough to describe how it is to be at the Nature Agro Resort, visit us, and feel nature..
